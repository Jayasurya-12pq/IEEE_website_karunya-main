Privacy Policy


Introduction

Karunya IEEE SB is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information provided by users on this website.


Data Collection

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other relevant details through our website for the purpose of organizing workshops, seminars, and other IEEE-related activities. In some instances, data may also be collected via third-party platforms utilized by IEEE Karunya SB.


Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for the purposes of IEEE Karunya SB's activities, such as event organization, communication, and reporting. The data is stored on highly secure servers managed by Karunya University and is not sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.


User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal information stored in our database or within any third-party platform used by IEEE Karunya SB. Requests can be made through the contact information provided on our website.


Security Measures

We employ advanced security measures to protect the personal information of our users. The data is stored under high-security surveillance and is maintained by a dedicated team within Karunya University.


Cookies

This website does not use, collect, or store cookies. No user data is tracked through cookies on this website.


Minors

There is no separate policy for minors as this website does not contain any content harmful to children.


Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other relevant laws of India. Karunya IEEE SB ensures compliance with these laws in the collection, storage, and use of personal information.
